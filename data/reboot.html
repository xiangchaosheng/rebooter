Reboot your Device

You can reboot your router now by clicking 'confirm' below. This will perform a reboot cycle by cutting the power to your router for a few seconds before turning it back on.

A reboot cycle may take a few minutes, after which point Router Rebooter will reconnect to your router.

Use the LEDs on your Router Rebooter to check its progress.

  • Blinking Red LED:
    Waiting to connect your Router
  • Blinking Blue LED:
    Connection established with your Router, Internet monitoring OFF
  • Solid Blue LED:
    Connection established with your Router, Internet monitoring ON
