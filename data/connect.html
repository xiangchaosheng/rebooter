Connect to your Router

Tap 'Confirm' below to connect your Router Rebooter to your Router's Wi-Fi. It may take a few minutes for Router Rebooter to establish a connection.

Use the LEDs on your Router Rebooter to check its progress.

  • Blinking Red LED:
    Waiting to connect your Router
  • Blinking Blue LED:
    Connection established with your Router, Internet monitoring OFF
  • Solid Blue LED:
    Connection established with your Router, Internet monitoring ON

Once a connection is established, access your Rebooter's Dashboard from your mobile devic within 20 minutes:

  • iOS: connect your mobile device to your router. Go to http://zobox.local from your Safari.
  • iOS/Android: re-connect your mobile device to your Rebooter's Wi-Fi (Zobox-ABCD), and then go to http://192.168.4.1 from your Safari/Chrome.

Visit http://www.zobox.xyz/help for more help.
